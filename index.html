Demographics

Our target market, primarily in rural areas with active agriculture, consists of small to medium-scale farmers with the means to invest in improving their practices. They're open to adopting new technologies, needing minimal education for integration. Our product offers ease of use and versatility, serving various crop types effortlessly.

Customer Reach

WCustomer Reach: We engage with local NGOs to educate farmers on agtech benefits, fostering trust through demonstrations. Aligning with government programs, we customize solutions to local conditions. Cost-effective digital marketing further boosts sales and revenue.

Advanced sensors and analytics provide valuable data insights to optimize irrigation schedules and crop management.
